## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Authentication Data

To provide our notification service, we require users to authenticate. We collect:

* **Email Address:** When you sign up via Magic Link, we collect your email address.
* **GitHub Profile Information:** If you choose to sign in with GitHub, we receive your GitHub username, full name, profile picture and email address associated with your account.

### B. Subscription Preferences

We store which applications you have chosen to subscribe to so that we can send you relevant updates.

### C. Technical Logs

Like most web services, our hosting providers may collect standard technical data, such as IP addresses, browser types and timestamps to ensure security and site performance.
